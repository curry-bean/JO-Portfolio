REJUVENATE PLANNING PRACTICE IN KENYA

Support Engagement of Registered Planning Firms

Our Achievements

o Held 2 Consultative Workshops with planning firms to enhance planning practice in Kenya;
o Registered 15 Planning Firms;
o Engagement of Planning Firms in preparation of the Murang'a University of Technology Master Plan;
o Facilitated KIP Registered firms to get regional engagement;
o Lobbied County Governments to budget for and procure planning consultancy services (Migori, Lamu, Embu, Garissa, Kiambu, Bungoma);
o Elicited support from firms in the completion of Embarigo Primary School Master Plan;
o Showcased Plans by firms to relevant clientele (KIP Conventions and Conferences, Devolution Conference, JABEIC Conference);
o Ensured involvement of planning firms in outreach to County Governments; and
o Held successful CPD targeting planning firms (Public Procurement, GIS).

Intention

o Support the formation of a Practicing Planners Forum and Association within KIP;
o Conduct solution-oriented consultative Workshops with Planning firms;
o Enhance the Registration, Recognition, and Recommendation of Valid Practicing Firms;
o Undertake the County Planning and Development project with support from planning firms;
o Develop guidelines on Planning methodology, inputs, timelines, and minimum and maximum costing on all statutorily required plans;
o Lobby for tendering of all spatial Planning and Development Projects by Planning authorities and relevant Government Agencies;
o Lobby for engagement of planning firms in Ward Development Plans and review of CIDP;
o Conduct regular Peer reviews of Planning projects and support progression and payment of reviewed ongoing planning assignments;
o Lobby CoG, County Governments on clearance of pending bills owed to planning firms;
o Advocate for quota system in an award of planning projects to valid planning firms;
o Lobby for Joint venture of Experienced Firms with emerging firms;
o Conduct relevant CPD and ReTooling workshops targeting planning firms (Taxation, Project Management, GIS Application);
o Lobby, inform and encourage joint venture application of planning firms to infrastructure development and regional planning projects; and
o Showcase Exemplary Plans by firms on the national, regional, and global stage for recognition and Award (KIP Conventions, AfriCities Summits, CAP, ISOCARP, WUF, COP).

Rejuvenate Planning by Governments

Our Achievements

o Achieved County Planning and Development Project with commitments to support Garissa, Vihiga, Kisii, Makueni, Embu, and Kiambu Counties;
o Lobbied for employment of close to 50 qualified planners in National Government agencies and County Governments (NLC, SHUD, Nakuru, Kisumu, Lamu, Kiambu);
o Lobbied for appointment of Planners in County Executive positions (Nakuru County);
o Successfully lobbied for KIP membership subscription and Conference participation facilitation of Planners in Government (Makueni, Nakuru, Machakos, Vihiga);
o Activated regional branches to support planners in Counties (Pwani, South Rift, and Western);
o Held successful CPD targeting planners in public service (Planning and land laws);
o Encouraged engagement of planners in relevant public agencies and sectors (KURA, KeRRA, FAO, UN-HABITAT, ITDG); and
o Partnered with CoG in the induction of County Directors of Lands and Planning.

Intention

o Lobby for the development of a National Development Planning Policy;
o Lobby for the promotion of planners in Government;
o Trigger Planning opportunities relevant to National Ministries, County agencies, and Municipalities;
o Support relevant experience and registration of planners in county Governments through the County Planning and Development project;
o Champion adequate facilitation planners in public service to supervise Planning Projects awarded to Valid Planning firms;
o Champion for Gainful Engagement of registered planners, KIP Graduates, and Student interns in Planning authorities and relevant Government Agencies;
o Lobby for KIP membership subscription and Conference participation facilitation of Planners in Government by the respective Employer;
o Lobby for the preparation of a comprehensive National Spatial Plan;
o Review planning manuals and Guidelines for the preparation of statutory Development plans;
o Support finalization of the Planning Handbook with Regional and County Specific recommendations;
o Support professional and academic progression of planners in public service (Training, Workshops, Scholarships, and exchange programs in CAP and CEAPN) and
o Support the presentation of Planning solutions by the government on the national, regional, and global stage for recognition and Award (KIP Conventions, AfriCities Summits, CAP, ISOCARP, WUF, COP).

Encourage Active Research in Planning Schools

Our Achievements

o Encouraged the research by UoN-DURP on Land fragmentation in Kenya;
o Partnering with on Ward Development Plans; and
o Elicited support towards Marine Spatial Planning short course in planning schools (WIOMSA, Pwani University, Maseno University)
Intention

o Incorporate Planning Schools in the County Planning and Development Project;
o Lobby for student exchange programs in regional and global programs (CAP, CPEAN);
o Encourage participation of planning schools in planning assignments by Planning firms;
o Lobby for support to planning schools to develop local context course outlines and content;
o Collaborate with Planning schools in active research on relevant issues (Sustainable Urbanisation, Affordable, and Social Housing, Food Security and Ecosystems Management, Integrated Refugee Settlements in Kenya); and
o Participate in and Support planning schools studio development.
o Support showcase of Planning School research and student outputs on a national, regional, and global stage for recognition and Award (KIP Conventions, CAP, ISOCARP, WUF, COP).
